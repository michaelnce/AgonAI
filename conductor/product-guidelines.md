# Product Guidelines

## Visual Identity
- **Design System:** Modern, dashboard-centric interface based on the "Debate Arena" design.
- **Theming:** Full support for both **Dark Mode** (default, as seen in `UI_Idea/screen.png`) and **Light Mode**.
- **Color Palette:**
  - Primary (Proponent): Blue (#3b82f6) with thunderbolt iconography.
  - Secondary (Opponent): Purple (#a855f7) with sparkle iconography.
  - Backgrounds: Deep slate/near-black for dark mode; clean white/light gray for light mode.
- **Typography:** Clean, sans-serif fonts (e.g., Inter, Geist) with clear hierarchical contrast for agent names and timestamps.

## Tone & Messaging
- **Debate Style:** Punchy, concise, and logically rigorous. Avoid long-form monologues.
- **Agent Voices:** 
  - Proponent: Constructive, assertive, and evidence-based.
  - Opponent: Critical, sharp, and focused on logical fallacies/risks.
  - Moderator: Objective, authoritative, and synthesizing.
- **User Interface:** Professional, technical, and data-driven ("Live Status", "Decision Matrix", "Stress Test").

## UI/UX Principles
- **Real-time Feedback:** Use token-by-token streaming for debate responses.
- **Clarity:** Distinct visual separation between Proponent and Opponent arguments (left vs. right alignment or color-coded blocks).
- **Accessibility:** High contrast ratios in both themes; clear labeling for interactive elements.
