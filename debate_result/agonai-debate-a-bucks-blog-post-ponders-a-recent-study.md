**Direct Engagement:** Casey consistently engaged with Leon's specific points and turned them around. **Evidence Quality:** Casey deployed a wider and more specific evidence base: the Princeton study, the Killingsworth-Kahneman 2023 reconciliation, the Harvard Study of Adult Development (Waldinger), Viktor Frankl's account, James Stockdale's POW experience, Sonja Lyubomirsky's 40% intentional activity finding, and direct quotations from Marcus Aurelius and Epictetus.
